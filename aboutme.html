I'm a Designer based in San Francisco.


Geometry, color / psychological concepts, and innovative tools seeking - those are the aspects that moves me in the design world. I believe that by using the basic principles of the design and by being multidisciplinary it is possible to develop any product (from underwears to aeroplanes).

My designer journey

- A history board of how I became the person and designer I am today -

1988 - I was born in Brazil
I was born in a seaside town on the northeast of Brazil, even though I spent almost my entire life in a countryside city, Campina Grande.
1996 - I got in my first Drawing classes
I always loved to draw and to make handcrafts, so I got in my first Drawing and painting classes as a kid.
1998 - I lived in New Jersey during one year
I moved to New Jersey with my parents and sister. My teacher at the time, Mr. Macy, suggested that I came back to the EUA to study Arts.
2007 - My freshman year in Design College
I began to study Industrial Design in the Federal University of Campina Grande - the human-centered design was always present in our daily life as students.
2009 - I was finalist in a national furniture design context
I was selected one of the five best furniture in a national design contest.
- Ergonomics background -
I also had my first teaching experience as a teacher assistant in Design Applied Ergonomics.
2010 - I was a trainee in Fashion Design
I had my first professional experience in the market as a trainee. The company produces naturally colored cotton - innovative sustainable technology that provides four different colors of fabrics without use of artificial dyeing. There I developed a small collection.
2011 - My graduation
I finished my undergraduate in Industrial Design - I developed a kitchen utensil with emotional design concepts for my final thesis.
- Interior Design background -
I became trainee as Interior Designer in a modular furniture company , it was a interesting experience for me since I had to deal directly with clients to solve their problems related to their rooms layout.
2012 - I had my first job that improved my organization capacity
I was a Junior Designer at a farm machinery factory, I worked improving and redesign parts of the products to achieve better production processes and optimize the product's usability while reducing costs. I also reorganized the material used by the workers to manufacture the products seeking for a better understanding of the process by them.
- Sustainability background -
At that same year I participate of a summer class in the Federal University of Parana about sustainability where I developed with my classmates group an article about a non-profit organization that shared books in recycling box.
2014 - Eurotrip: Germany, Holland and England!
Getting in touch with things I’ve seen in Art History class inspired me! Specially around the Bauhaus school.
2015 - I began my UI/UX journey
I started at the Distributed Systems Laboratory, there I worked in many innovative and social engajing projects. Working together with developers, I used prototypes to improve the communication of my ideas, while learning their technical language including CSS and HTML through the codeacademy.com online course.
- A foot in the fashion -
At that time, I decided to take also fashion construction classes. That is something I’ve always been passionate, as I have grown up in my grandmother’s seam atelie.
2016 - Me and my friends won a national app context
This contest was hold by the Brazilian Government. The goal was to develop apps to help the fight against the corruption. That is a boring topic for the majority of the population, as we found a fun way to deal with it with our app - As Diferentonas. Today Brazilians can follow where the public money is being spent in easier way filtering the data and also being able to report illegal situations.
2017 - I moved to San Francisco
For me, it is a dream coming true to live in a city full of history and different cultures, having the opportunity to live new experiences and to change as a person. Since I arrived, I did things that inspired me and opened my mind as a designer - Advanced drawing classes at SFAI and Fashion construction classes at AAU and Master in Design at SFSU.
